Still Willing to Try

Sophy, this isn’t just a website. It’s a window into my heart.

Dear Sophy,

I don’t know if you’ll read this. I don’t know if you’ll smile, cry, or close the tab. But I needed to say this somewhere that felt real. Somewhere that felt like you.

I’ve had time to think. Time to feel the silence. Time to realize that what we had wasn’t ordinary—it was rare. And rare things deserve to be fought for.

I’m not perfect. I’ve made mistakes. I’ve let fear speak louder than love. But I’ve grown. I’ve learned. And through it all, my heart keeps coming back to you.

I’m still willing to try. To listen. To learn. To love you better.

If there’s even a flicker of hope in you, I’ll be here.

No pressure. No expectations. Just a quiet, steady hope.

Yours,
Chidi
